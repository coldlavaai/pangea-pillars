HSE shows up.

- Has CSCS card? ✅
- Available when needed? ✅
- Location works? ✅

Qualified applicants proceed.

Unqualified ones get filtered out automatically.

No human time wasted.

AI bot requests documents:

**Bot:** "Send a photo of your CSCS card"

**Bot:** "Send a photo of your passport or ID"

AI verifies:

- Right-to-work status
- Document authenticity

Time: 1 minute.

AI bot sends H&S induction video link.

Signs digitally at the end.

Completion tracked automatically.

Manager sees real-time update: "John Smith completed H&S induction."

Manager sees dashboard:

- Qualified applicants

Time: 10 seconds.

### Step 6: Worker Gets Site Details

Worker receives automated WhatsApp message:

- Contact: [Site manager name + phone]
- Dress code: PPE required

Automated reminders sent:

- Day before shift
- 2 hours before start time

Result: No-shows reduced 60-80%.

### Step 7: Worker Clocks In

Worker arrives on site.

Clocks in via WhatsApp link.

GPS verifies location.

Manager sees real-time update: "John Smith clocked in at 7:02 AM."

Total time from application to site-ready: 2 minutes (worker time).
