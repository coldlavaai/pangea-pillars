## Right-to-Work Compliance for Construction

### Legal Requirements

UK law (gov.uk) requires right-to-work checks for all employees.

Employers must verify:

- Passport (UK or valid visa)
- Biometric Residence Permit
- Birth certificate + National Insurance (for UK citizens)

Checks must be done before employment starts.

Documents must be copied and stored.

Proof of check = protection.

No proof = liability.
